Tijuana Flats

Tijuana Flats is Tex-Mex restaurant popular for it's hot suace bar. On Tuesday they have 2 tacos, chips and a drink for $5.00.

Chick-fil-a

Chick-fil-a is popular for their chicken sandwiches. They don't sell any beef. They basically have the best fast food chicken.

Sonic

Sonic is mainly known for their drink options. You can basically have any drink you want. The Reese's and banana milkshake is awesome.

Steak n Shake

Steak n Shake has pretty great burgers. Their food is extremely greasy and unhealthy, but their open all night, so it's awesome for late night meals.
